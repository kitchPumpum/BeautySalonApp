About this location
Beauty Salon Suite offers fully furnished, move-in ready private suites to independent beauty and wellness professionals looking to grow their business and improve their lives. Join our community of creative and inspiring entrepreneurs today! Schedule a no-obligation tour to learn about our space and how we help professionals like you meet your full financial and artistic potential.

Conveniently located in the hamlet of East Meadow alongside bustling anchor stores Stew Leonard’s and Marshalls, Beauty Salon Suite East Meadow is a new beauty and wellness complex offering luxury services to a discerning clientele. The newly renovated Stew Leonard’s Plaza has plenty of parking and is nestled at the corner of Front Street and East Meadow Avenue, just south of Eisenhower Park and Hempstead Turnpike.

An abundance of great shopping, fitness and dining options are within a stone’s throw, including Club Pilates, Rite Aid, Uncle Giuseppe’s Marketplace, Garden Social, Orange Theory Fitness, Chipotle, SoBol, Dunkin’, Sonic, Texas Roadhouse and Starbucks. Plus there’s a wide array of stores and eateries in the nearby Roosevelt Field and Samanea New York (formerly The Source) shopping areas: the heart of Nassau County’s retail corridor.

Beauty Salon Suite East Meadow is centrally located to major parkways, including the Northern State, Meadowbrook, Wantagh and Southern State, making driving from any place on Long Island a snap.
